# Returns Policy

This document defines the official rules and procedures for handling product returns. It is structured to be clear for customers and operationally consistent for support teams.

## Return window
- Customers may return products within **30 days from the delivery date**.
- After this period, items are generally not eligible for return unless covered by an exception.

## Eligible items
- **Non-perishable products** in unused condition and original packaging.
- Items purchased directly from the official store with a valid receipt or order ID.

## Not eligible
- **Perishable products** such as fresh produce, food, and beverages.  
  Certain **sealed, shelf-stable perishable products** (e.g., ground coffee, tea, cocoa powder) may qualify for return under the exceptions below.
- **Personal hygiene and care products** (e.g., toothbrushes, toothpaste, soaps) if opened or used.
- **Customized or made-to-order products**.

## Exceptions
Returns are accepted under the following conditions:
- The item arrived **defective** or **damaged** during delivery (photo evidence required).
- The item received is **incorrect** compared to the order confirmation.
- **Sealed, shelf-stable perishable items** (e.g., ground coffee, tea, cocoa powder) are eligible for return **within 7 days of delivery** **if unopened and sealed**. Opened items are not eligible unless defective.

## Refund policy
- Approved returns will be refunded to the **original payment method**.
- Refunds are processed within **7–10 business days** after the returned item passes inspection.
- Shipping fees are generally **non-refundable** unless the error was on the company’s side.

## Exchanges
- Direct exchanges are **not supported**.
- Customers must return the original product and place a new order separately.

## Process
For eligible returns, the standard process consists of three steps:
1. **Confirm eligibility** based on product type and delivery date.
2. **Provide a return shipping label** (sent digitally to the customer).
3. **Issue a refund** within 7–10 business days after inspection.

## Customer support
- If eligibility is unclear or assistance is needed, a support representative will help.
- Contact channels include **email** and **chat support** listed on the official website.
