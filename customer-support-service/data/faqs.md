# Customer Support FAQs

This document provides clear, customer-oriented guidance on orders, shipping, returns, and special situations. It reflects the official policies and service standards of the store.

## 1. Orders and Shipping

### 1.1 How can I check my order status?

You can track your order using the **tracking ID** included in your order confirmation email. Common status updates include:

- **Processing** – Your items are being prepared.
- **In transit** – Your order is on the way and an **estimated delivery date** (ETA) is provided.
- **Out for delivery** – Your order is expected to arrive today.
- **Delivered** – The package has been delivered.
- **Delayed** – Delivery is taking longer than expected, with the reason usually stated.

**Tip:** If your order shows as delivered but you cannot locate it, refer to section 1.2.

### 1.2 What should I do if my package shows as “Delivered” but I can’t find it?

1. Verify that the **delivery address** on your order confirmation is correct.  
2. Check common delivery areas such as porches, lobbies, or mailrooms, and ask neighbors or building staff.  
3. Allow **24 hours**, as some carriers mark deliveries ahead of time.  
4. If the package is still missing, contact Customer Support with your **tracking ID** for assistance.

### 1.3 Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Address changes after checkout are not guaranteed. If your order is still **Processing**, contact Customer Support immediately. Once it is **In transit**, only limited carrier adjustments may be possible.

## 2. Returns and Refunds

Our return policy applies to all purchases made through the official store and aims to ensure a fair and sustainable shopping experience.

For complete details, please refer to the **Returns Policy**. The following summarizes the most frequent cases.

### 2.1 What is the standard return window?

Products can be returned within **30 days** from the **delivery date**, provided they are **non-perishable**, **unused**, and in their **original packaging**.

### 2.2 Can perishable items be returned?

- Generally, **perishable products** such as fresh food and produce are **not eligible** for return.  
- **Exception:** Certain **sealed, shelf-stable items** such as **Organic Coffee**, **Organic Tea**, or **Cocoa Powder** can be returned **within 7 days** if **unopened and sealed**.

### 2.3 What should I do if my item arrives damaged or defective?

Items that are **defective**, **damaged during delivery**, or **incorrect** compared to your order confirmation are eligible for return. Photo evidence may be required. Shipping fees will be refunded if the issue originated from the company.

### 2.4 Do you offer exchanges?

Direct exchanges are not available. Eligible items can be returned for a refund, after which a new order may be placed.

### 2.5 How long does it take to receive a refund?

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** within **7 to 10 business days** after the returned item passes inspection.

### 2.6 Return eligibility examples

- **Non-perishable hygiene items** (e.g., *Bamboo Toothbrush*, *Aloe Vera Gel*):  
  Eligible within **30 days** if **unused** and in **original packaging**. Opened hygiene items are **not eligible**.  
- **Non-perishable home and kitchen items** (e.g., *Glass Food Containers*, *Ceramic Mug*):  
  Eligible within **30 days** if unused and in original packaging.  
- **Perishable products** (e.g., *Organic Apples*, *Organic Bananas*):  
  **Not eligible** for return unless damaged upon arrival, supported by photo evidence.  
- **Sealed, shelf-stable perishable goods** (e.g., *Organic Coffee*, *Organic Tea*, *Cocoa Powder*):  
  Eligible **within 7 days** if **unopened and sealed**. Opened items are **not eligible** unless defective.

### 2.7 How can I start a return?

1. Have your **tracking ID** and **product information** ready.  
2. Customer Support will confirm **eligibility** and provide a **prepaid return label** if applicable.  
3. Pack the items securely and include all accessories, manuals, and original packaging.

## 3. Product Information

### 3.1 What is a product code (SKU)?

A **SKU** is a unique identifier assigned to each product. It helps ensure accurate order management, product lookup, and return processing. The SKU is displayed on your order confirmation and on product packaging.

### 3.2 How can I verify product eligibility or details?

- **Order information** such as delivery date and product names appears in your order confirmation and account history.  
- **Product-specific information** like category or return eligibility is available on each product’s detail page or in the Returns Policy.

## 4. Packaging and Return Process

### 4.1 How should I prepare my return package?

Use the original packaging whenever possible. Include all accessories, manuals, and labels. Returned items must arrive in good, resalable condition unless they are being returned for damage or defect.

### 4.2 Who pays for return shipping?

- If the **wrong item** was shipped or the item was **damaged or defective**, return shipping costs are covered by the company.  
- For discretionary returns on **eligible items**, shipping fees may be deducted from the refund depending on the case.

## 5. Special Situations

### 5.1 Orders with mixed items (perishable and non-perishable)

Only eligible items can be returned. Customer Support will guide you through the process and provide separate labels if necessary.

### 5.2 Gift orders

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**. To request assistance, please provide the **tracking ID** or the **email address** used for the purchase.

### 5.3 Promotions and bundles

If the returned item was part of a promotion or bundle, the refund may be **prorated** according to the promotional terms.

## 6. Contact and Support

For any assistance, please reach out through the contact options listed on the official website. Include your **tracking ID** and product details for a faster resolution.
